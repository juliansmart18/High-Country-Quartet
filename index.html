Welcome

Greetings,

We are the High Country String Quartet, a versatile ensemble dedicated to delivering musical excellence in Western North Carolina. Our members bring a wealth of classical training to the stage, but our commitment to musical diversity goes beyond convention.

Our repertoire spans a wide spectrum of styles, catering to an array of events and settings, with a predominant presence at weddings. Beyond the realms of classical music, we delight in infusing a touch of diversity into our performances. Be it popular chart-toppers, cherished folk melodies, selections from the silver screen and theater, or even a dash of the jazzy allure, we are well-prepared to meet your musical preferences.

From elegant weddings to lively celebrations, the High Country String Quartet is here to provide an unforgettable musical experience for your special occasion.
